Take a Class at Hack Manhattan

  • September 26, 2013
  • Uncategorized
  • Dave
  • Leave a comment
  • 1

Fresh from meeting hundreds of new faces at World Maker Faire this year, we’re excited to offer several workshops to our members and visitors in the coming months.

 

Learn to use Autodesk Fusion for 3D CAD for 3D printing

KiCad Workshop

OpenSCAD Workshop

OpenSCAD Workshop

Basic Arduino Class

 

All are very reasonably priced, and taught by hand-picked instructors. If you’ve been thinking of visiting us, a workshop is a great way to see the space and get stuck in with learning and making something new in our community. See you there, or at any other event or open house on our calendar!
