Super Tech Tuesday was, well, Super!

  • August 26, 2012
  • Uncategorized
  • Crystal
  • Leave a comment
  • 0

Thanks to everyone who came out for Super Tech Tuesday! We had 3D scanning and printing, electronics triage, HF amateur radio, toner transfer circuit etching, and visits from NYCSats and Fixers Collective NYC.

See the original Facebook Event listing here.

3D scans captured using the Kinect and rendered using ReconstructMe software.

Dave and Ron at the 3D scanning station, using Kinect and ReconstructMe to do head scans.

Dave printing the 3D scan of Barry's head on a Mendel RepRap using PLA plastic.

Visitors making use of our hot air rework station to do some circuit repairs.

A charlieplexed circuit designed by Rob, laser printed onto catalog stock for heat transfer to a copper laminate base.

NYCSats working on their project.

Justin explains how the vertical hydroponic garden works.

The beginnings of an underwater remote operated vehicle project.

Dave's homebrew computer, put together out of spare bits he had lying around.

Chris' remote control helicopter!
